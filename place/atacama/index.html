Atacama

The Atacama Desert is the driest hot desert on Earth, and stretches over 1000km of Chile and Peru west of the Andes. The brackish Lake Miscanti is visible in the center, with its smaller sibling Miñiques Lake to the south. The volcanoes of Miñiques and Cerro Miscanti are on the edge of the scene.
