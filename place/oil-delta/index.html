Oil Delta

The Tigris and Euphrates Rivers (foundation of the “Fertile Crescent”) combine north of the Iraqi city of Basrah to form the ”Shatt al-Arab”. The waterway forms the Iran-Iraq border and empties into the Persian Gulf. The Kuwaiti island of Bubiyan island is visible on the western side, as well as Marsh Arab settlements lining the waterway in the center.
